Nearby Places To Visit

1000 Pillars Temple

The 1000 Pillars Temple is famous for its stunning architecture with around 1000 intricately carved pillars. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, it's a revered pilgrimage site known for its beauty and spirituality, attracting visitors worldwide.

Distance: 8 min (2.3 km) via Machili Bazar Rd

Kakatiya Musical Garden

The Kakatiya Musical Garden is a lovely place to visit. It has beautiful flowers and plants. The best part is the musical fountain. It dances to the music and lights up at night. People enjoy watching it. You can also take a peaceful walk or have a picnic there. It's a nice spot for families and friends to spend time together.

Distance: 11 min (3.5 km) via New Bus Stand Rd

Ekashila Lake

Ekashila Lake is a serene oasis in the heart of the city, cherished for its peaceful ambiance and scenic beauty. Surrounded by lush greenery, it's a popular spot for relaxation and contemplation, drawing visitors seeking solace amidst urban life's hustle and bustle.

Distance: 22 min (9.7 km) via Hunter Rd

Warangal Fort

The Warangal Fort is an old and important place. It was built a long time ago by the Kakatiya dynasty. People can visit it and see the big walls and gates. It's like stepping back in time to learn about the past. The fort has a lot of history and stories to tell. Visitors can enjoy the view from the top and imagine what life was like many years ago.

Distance: 20 min (8.7 km) via Hunter Rd
