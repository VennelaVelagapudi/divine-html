Nearby Places To Visit

NTR Gardens

NTR Gardens is a serene oasis nestled within the city. With its winding pathways, colorful flower beds, and tranquil water features, it offers a welcome escape from the hustle and bustle of urban life. Visitors can stroll through the gardens, unwind by the ponds, or simply bask in the natural beauty all around.

Distance: 9 min (3.6 km) via NTR Marg

Lumbini Park

Lumbini Park is a beautiful spot where people go to relax and enjoy nature. It's a peaceful place with gardens, ponds, and sometimes even musical fountains. People often visit to spend time with family and friends, have picnics, or just take a leisurely stroll. It's a lovely place to unwind and appreciate the outdoors.

Distance: 6 min (1.7 km) via NH 44

Hussain Sagar

Hussain Sagar is a large lake with a famous statue of Buddha in its center. The statue stands tall, attracting many visitors who come to admire its serene presence. Surrounding the lake are beautiful parks and promenades where people often gather to relax and enjoy the scenic views. The lake serves as a popular recreational spot for locals and tourists alike, offering boating and other water activities.

Distance: 10 min (3.6 km) via NTR Marg

Birla Science Centre

The Birla Science Centre is a place where people can learn about science in a fun way. It has lots of cool things to see and do, like interactive exhibits and planetarium shows. You can explore different areas of science, from outer space to the human body. It's a great place for both kids and adults to discover and enjoy science together.

Distance: 1 min (200.0 m) via Naubat Pahad Ln and Birla Mandir Rd
