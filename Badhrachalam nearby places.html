Nearby Places To Visit

The Sri Sita Ramachandra Swamy Temple

The Sri Sita Ramachandra Swamy Temple near Badhrachalam is a special place where people worship Lord Rama, Sita, and Lakshmana. Many visit to pray and feel close to these gods. It's a place full of faith and peace, drawing people from different places to find blessings and solace.

Distance: 6 min (1.9 km) via NH 30

Sabari

Sabari, near Badhrachalam, is a special place in Hindu stories. It's where a devotee named Sabari lived. She waited for Lord Rama with love and devotion. People visit Badhrachalam to remember her story and feel close to Rama..

Distance: 6 min (1.9 km) via NH 30

Parnasala

Parnasala is an important place in Hindu stories. It's where Lord Rama, his wife Sita, and his brother Lakshmana lived in a simple leaf hut during their time in the forest. Many people visit Parnasala to feel close to the spirit of Rama and to enjoy the peaceful surroundings. It's a special spot that reminds people of the power of devotion and simplicity.

Distance: 46 min (34.0 km) via SH 12

Papi Kondalu

Papi Kondalu is a beautiful place with tall hills, green forests, and a winding river. People love to visit here for boat rides, enjoying the waterfalls and trees. It's a perfect spot for nature lovers and adventurers.

Distance: 2 min (450.0 m) via NH 30
