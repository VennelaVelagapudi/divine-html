Nearby Places To Visit

Bath Ghat

A serene spot nestled amidst lush surroundings, Bath Ghat offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. With its soothing ambiance and natural beauty, it beckons visitors to unwind and rejuvenate their spirits. The gentle sounds of flowing water and the rustle of leaves create a peaceful atmosphere, making it the perfect place for quiet contemplation or simply enjoying the beauty of nature.

Distance: 4 min (1.5 km) via step down to main Rd/temple down Rd

Nakshatra and Rasi Vanam

Gowsala Srikalahasti is a place where people gather to worship and find peace. It's a spiritual hub, where visitors connect with their faith through rituals and prayers. The ambiance is serene, with devotees often engaging in meditation or contemplation. It holds significance for many, serving as a sanctuary for spiritual seekers seeking solace and enlightenment.

Distance: 7 min (2.4 km) via Annavaram Town Rd

Central Park

In the heart of a bustling city lies a sprawling green oasis where people gather to relax, play, and connect with nature. This tranquil retreat offers wide-open spaces, shady trees, and winding pathways perfect for strolling or picnicking. It's a beloved destination where families, friends, and solo adventurers alike come to unwind and enjoy the simple pleasures of outdoor life.

Distance: 4 min (600 m) via unnamed roads

Sun Dial

The sundial at Annavaram is a simple yet fascinating device that uses the sun's position to tell time. Located in Annavaram, India, this ancient timekeeping tool consists of a flat plate with markings and a gnomon, which casts a shadow onto the plate. As the sun moves across the sky, the shadow changes, indicating the time of day. It's a beautiful blend of nature and technology, offering a timeless way to track the passage of time.

Distance: 7 min (2.3 km) via step down to main Rd/temple down Rd and Annavaram Town Rd
