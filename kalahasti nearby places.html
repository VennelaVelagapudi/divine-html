Nearby Places To Visit

NTR Park

Nestled within tranquil surroundings, this park serves as a tranquil haven where families unwind and friends gather for leisurely strolls. Just nearby, a sacred temple beckons worshippers, echoing with prayers and spiritual resonance.

Distance: 10 min (2.8 km) via Tada Kalahasti Rd

Gowsala

Gowsala Srikalahasti is a place where people gather to worship and find peace. It's a spiritual hub, where visitors connect with their faith through rituals and prayers. The ambiance is serene, with devotees often engaging in meditation or contemplation. It holds significance for many, serving as a sanctuary for spiritual seekers seeking solace and enlightenment.

Distance: 11 min (2.7 km) via Tada Kalahasti Rd and Sannidhi St

Kanappa Temple

Kanappa Temple is a sacred site known for its deep spiritual significance. It's a place where devotees gather to express their faith and devotion. The temple holds rituals and ceremonies that are central to the beliefs of its followers.

Distance: 12 min (3.1 km) via Tada Kalahasti Rd and Sannidhi St

Baradwaja Theertham

Baradwaja Theertham is a revered site known for its spiritual significance. Many people visit this sacred place to seek blessings and purification. It holds deep meaning for those who believe in its spiritual power, drawing pilgrims from far and wide to partake in its rituals and ceremonies.

Distance: 10 min (2.5 km) via Tada Kalahasti Rd and Sannidhi St
