Nearby Places To Visit

Surendrapuri

Bhuvanagiri Fort is an ancient stronghold with a rich history. It served as a strategic defense point and witnessed many battles throughout the centuries. The fort's sturdy walls and imposing architecture stand as a testament to its significance in the region's past.

Distance: 23 min (18.7 km) via NH163 and NH 161AA

Lotus Temple

The Lotus Temple is a peaceful place for people to come together and pray, shaped like a blooming flower. Yadadri is a sacred site where devotees go to worship and seek blessings. Both places offer serenity and spiritual connection.

Distance: 27 min (21.6 km) via Yadagirigutta Rd, NH163 and NH 161AA

Kolanupaka Temple

Kolanupaka in Yadadri district is famous for its ancient Jain temple, showcasing intricate architecture and religious significance. The town holds historical importance and attracts visitors interested in exploring its cultural heritage.

Distance: 45 min (39.8 km) via NH163

Bhuvanagiri Fort

Bhuvanagiri is a town steeped in history and culture. It's famous for its historical monuments, including forts and temples, which reflect the region's vibrant past. The town attracts visitors interested in exploring its heritage and experiencing its traditional way of life.

Distance: 15 min (9.6 km) via NH 161AA
