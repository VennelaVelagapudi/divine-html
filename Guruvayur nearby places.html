Nearby Places To Visit

Kapila Theertham

Kapila Theertham is a small Saivite Temple and Theertham, located at Tirupati in Tirupati district of Andhra Pradesh, India. The idol is believed to be installed by Kapila Muni and hence Lord Siva here is referred to as Kapileswara.

Distance: 11 min (3.6 km) via NH71

ISCKON, Tirupati

Popularly also known as Hare Rama Hare Krishna temple, located in the Ramachandra Nagar, on the Hare Rama Hare Krishna Road, ISKCON Tirupati Temple is a temple complex dedicated to Lord Krishna and located in the holy city of Tirupati.

Distance: 11 min (3.8 km) via Alipiri Rd

Zoological Park

The Sri Venkateswara Zoological Park, often referred to as Tirupati Zoo Park,has many different animals, like tigers and bears. It's famous for its efforts to take care of animals and teach people about them. You can visit the zoo with your family and learn about wildlife while enjoying nature walks and guided tours. It's a great place for anyone who loves animals!

Distance: 16 min (8.6 km) via NH71 and Alipiri Rd

Padmavati Ammavaru

The Padmavathi Ammavari Temple is a special Hindu temple for Goddess Padmavathi, who is married to Lord Venkateswara. Many people visit to pray and feel peaceful there. The temple looks beautiful, especially during festivals like Brahmotsavam.

Distance: 13 min (4.7 km) via Tiruchanoor Rd/Tirupati Arkonam Renigunta Pudi Rd
