Nearby Places To Visit

Konda Reddy Fort

Konda Reddy Fort is an old place with walls that tell stories about brave people from a long time ago. It's like a giant history book, reminding us of the battles and leaders of the past. Even though it's old and worn out, the fort still stands tall, keeping watch over the area with a quiet strength.

Distance: 4 hr 31 min (255.6 km) via NH40 and NH 67

Oravakallu Rock Garden

Orvakallu Rock Garden is a lovely place made from rocks. It's calm and beautiful, with big cliffs and nice views. You can walk around and enjoy nature here. It's a great spot to relax and have fun outside.

Distance: 3 hr 51 min (233.1 km) via NH40 and NH 67

Rollapadu Wildlife Santuary

Rollapadu Wildlife Sanctuary is a special place where many different plants and animals live together. It's like a safe home for them. The sanctuary helps protect these creatures and their environment. You can see all kinds of birds and animals here, enjoying their natural surroundings. It's a beautiful place for people to visit and learn about nature..

Distance: 4 hr 25 min (227.1 km) via NH40 and NH 67

Ranganayaka Swamy Temple

The Ranganayaka Swamy Temple is a beautiful place where people go to worship. It has amazing designs and big towers that make it special. People come here to pray and feel peaceful. Inside, priests perform rituals and people say prayers. It's an old place that has lots of history and tradition..

Distance: 2 hr 12 min (137.6 km) via NH565
