Nearby Places To Visit

Amaravati Sthupam

The Amaravati Stupa is a significant structure known for its ancient history and cultural importance. It stands as a testament to the architectural and artistic achievements of its time. The stupa is adorned with intricate carvings and sculptures, depicting various aspects of life and beliefs from that era. It serves as a reminder of the rich heritage and craftsmanship of ancient civilizations.

Distance: 3 min (750.0 m) via Amaralingeswara Swamy Temple Rd

Dhyana Buddha Statue

The Dhyana Buddha statue is a serene and majestic figure representing peace and meditation. Its tranquil posture and gentle expression inspire feelings of calmness and introspection. This statue symbolizes the essence of inner peace and spiritual enlightenment.

Distance: 1 hr (37.1 km) via Amaravathi Rd/Guntur - Amaravathi Rd

Amaravati Museum

The Amaravati Museum is a place where you can see many old and interesting things. It has artifacts and objects from the past, like sculptures and paintings. People visit the museum to learn about history and culture. It's a cool place to explore and discover new things!

Distance: 3 min (650.0 m) via Amaralingeswara Swamy Temple Rd

Amareswara Temple

The Amaravati Amareswara Temple is a sacred site dedicated to the deity Amareswara. It holds great significance for devotees who come to offer prayers and seek blessings. The temple is known for its architectural beauty and serene atmosphere, attracting visitors from far and wide. Devotional rituals and festivals are held regularly, fostering a sense of spirituality among worshippers.

Distance: 59 min (37.0 km) via Amaravathi Rd/Guntur - Amaravathi Rd
