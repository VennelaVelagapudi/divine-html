Nearby Places To Visit

Vyasa Maharshi Cave

Vyasa Maharshi Cave is a sacred place associated with the ancient sage Vyasa. It's a quiet spot where people go to meditate and seek wisdom. Legend says that Vyasa, who is famous for compiling the Hindu epic Mahabharata, used to meditate in this cave. It's a place of reverence and inspiration for those on a spiritual journey.

Distance: 2 min (650.0 m) via Gnana Saraswathi Temple Rd and Saraswathi Temple Rd

Sriram Sagar Reservoir

Sriram Sagar Reservoir is a big water reservoir. It stores water for people to use. It's important for farming and providing water to nearby areas. The reservoir also has a nice view where people can relax and enjoy nature. It's a good place to visit with family and friends, especially during sunny days.

Distance: 1 hr 22 min (53.5 km) via Basara Rd

Kuntala Waterfalls

Kuntala Waterfalls is a beautiful spot where water flows down from a height. It's a peaceful place surrounded by nature. People visit to enjoy the sight of water cascading down and the sound it makes. It's a refreshing experience, especially on hot days. Families often come here for picnics and to relax. It's a place where you can feel close to nature and forget about worries for a while.

Distance: 2 hr 36 min (113.4 km) via NH 61

Ashok Sagar/Jankampet Lake

Ashok Sagar, also known as Jankampet Lake, is a serene water body that offers a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. It's a popular spot for locals and tourists alike to relax and unwind. The lake's tranquil waters and scenic surroundings make it an ideal place for picnics, boat rides, and leisurely strolls along the shore. Visitors can enjoy the fresh air and natural beauty while watching birds or simply soaking in the calm atmosphere.

Distance: 38 min (25.2 km) via Basara Rd
